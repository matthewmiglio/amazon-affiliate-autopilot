About the Designer
Oscar de la Renta’s philosophy is distinguished by a timeless feminine aesthetic, expressed through exceptional artisanal details and embellishments. The brand relies on masterful artistry to uphold the highest standards for craftsmen. Our design studio and ateliers work according to the standards of European couture houses. Every design begins with a detailed sketch by the designers before passing through scores of expert hands, ensuring the same attention to detail is placed on the interior construction and flawless fit of each garment as on the exterior’s lavish fabrications, exquisite prints, and ornate embroideries. Pieces are crafted within the house’s New York City atelier and the finest workrooms in Europe.
