About the Designer
When MATEO’s eponymous brand formally launched in 2009, the focus was men’s jewelry, the first collection taking inspiration from the concept of a working man’s toolbox. MATEO refocused solely as a women’s fine jewelry collection in 2014, converging on an aesthetic of simplicity and minimalism, aiming to create modern art for the modern woman. Each piece is beautifully made in New York city of 14-karat gold with diamond and precious gemstone accents.
